About me

Welcome to my website! I’m Marta Maxia, from Cagliari, and I am a PhD student in Economics at Bocconi University.

My research interests lie at the intersection of microeconomics, behavioral economics, and the economics of science. Before starting my PhD, I received an MSc in Economic and Social Sciences from Bocconi University. I have worked as a Research Assistant in microeconomics at IGIER and in behavioral economics at the Bocconi Experimental Laboratory for the Social Sciences (BELSS).
